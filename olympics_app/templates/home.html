Welcome to the Olympic dashboard app!

This is an interactive web-app designed to show you interesting facts about the Olympics with data from Athens 1896 to Rio 2016!

You can use web-app for the following:

  • Want to see who's number one?
      • Check out the leaderboards.
  • Want to see who the silver medal winners of archery in the 1900?
      • Try our filter for participations where you can filter by medal, sex, sport and year.
  • Interested in the average height of male basketball gold medal winners in 2008? Or how many medals Denmark won compared to Sweden and Norway in specific years?
      • Play around with our interactive statistics module with a bar graph generator.
  • Do you want to search up a specific athlete, sport or year?
      • Use our search engine powered by regex!

    Data set used:

      120 years of olympic history athletes and results
